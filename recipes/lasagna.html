Lasagna Recipe!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozza / Riccotta Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Place lasagna noodles on bottom of glass baking dish.
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the noodles, then add a layer of cheese and meat.
  3. Repeat this process until the dish is full.
  4. Add a layer of mozza on top.
  5. Cover and bake on 325 degrees for 4 hours.
